CONSISTENCY AND PATIENCE ARE THE KEY

Hi - My Name is Mehdi Rahimi

I’m 19 years old and based in Erfurt, Germany. I was born in Afghanistan and moved to Germany at a young age, carrying with me both the resilience shaped by my past and the ambition to create my future. My story is one of constant adaptation — learning a new language, building new connections, and finding my place in a world completely different from where I started.
I study computer science, learn fast, and build on natural talent with hard work. I’m curious, focused, and always pushing forward — not just to get better, but to do things right.
I care about character. Respect, discipline, and honesty guide how I learn, work, and interact. My time in sports has taught me that both the mind and the body need discipline, patience, and consistent effort to truly grow.
This site shows who I am, what I do, and what I stand for. It’s not just a portfolio — it’s a reflection of my journey, my skills, and the mindset that drives me. I believe in pushing limits, embracing challenges, and never settling for “good enough” when I know I can do better.

Learn. Think. Improve

Brain – For me, the mind is the starting point of everything. A sharp and disciplined mind can turn obstacles into opportunities. I believe in staying curious, questioning what I hear, and always looking for a deeper understanding. With focus, patience, and the will to adapt, the mind becomes the strongest tool we have.

Strength. Health. Discipline

Body – Strength is more than muscle; it’s discipline, consistency, and respect for your limits. I believe in taking care of my body through training, movement, and a healthy lifestyle. A strong body supports a strong mind, and every drop of sweat is an investment in resilience and self-control.

Faith. Guidance. Peace

Spirit – For me, nothing is more important than my faith in Islam. It shapes my values, guides my actions, and keeps me grounded no matter where life takes me. My faith is my source of strength, peace, and purpose — the foundation upon which everything else in my life is built.
